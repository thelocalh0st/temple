About Us

Sri Venkateswara Swamy, as a meritorious result of our previous birth, lived in a mountain cave in the outskirts of Srinagar village at Maharamya place, 20 km from Bhagyanagar. In the distance, on the way to Srisailam, Mankhal, Tukkuguda, Imanguda, Sardarnagar, Ravirala, Saraswatiguda, Harshaguda villages are very close to Srimadakhilanda Koti Brahmandanayaka Bhaktajanakaraku Sri Adilakshmi Alumenga and Sri Balaji Venkateswara Swamy temple.
